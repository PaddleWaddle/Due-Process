How is this Right used in Schools?







This right is showed in school through suspension and expulsion. You are allowed to have a hearing and explain your side.

This came into effect when the Supreme Court ruled that you can’t get suspended without an understanding of your side.

“The Supreme Court rules that students cannot be suspended from school without a hearing and an opportunity to explain what happened from their perspective.” (1975)
